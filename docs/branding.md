# Branding & Launch Strategy — browse.pro.bd

## Branding Direction

### Platform Identity

**browse.pro.bd** is Bangladesh's **public showcase for developers and creators**.

## Brand Positioning

### Target Audience
- 🇧🇩 Bangladeshi developers
- 💻 Tech enthusiasts
- 🏢 Companies hiring in Bangladesh
- 📚 Students & learners

### Value Proposition
- **Discovery** — Find talented developers & cool projects
- **Showcase** — Get recognition for your work

- **Trust** — Verified, open-source focused

## Launch Strategy

### Pre-Launch (1-2 weeks before)

1. **Build community anticipation**
   - Tease on social media
   - Share vision in dev groups
   - Build early signup list

2. **Content gathering**
   - Reach out to known BD developers
   - Get 20-30 featured projects lined up
   - Highlight prominent creators

3. **Prepare materials**
   - Announcement post
   - Social media graphics
   - Email template

---

### Launch Day

Post to all channels simultaneously:

#### 1. **Facebook Dev Groups**
- Bangladesh Web Developers
- Bangladesh Programmers Community
- Tech Bangladesh
- React Bangladesh
- Django Bangladesh
- [other tech communities]

Post content:
```
🎉 Introducing browse.pro.bd!

A public showcase for Bangladeshi developers 
and open-source projects.

👉 Visit: browse.pro.bd
📝 Submit your project: [link]

We're launching with X amazing projects!
```

#### 2. **LinkedIn**
- Professional announcement
- Share platform vision
- Highlight featured creators
- CTA to submit

#### 3. **GitHub**
- Trending repositories (if possible)
- GitHub discussions/announcements
- Pin contribution guide

#### 4. **X/Twitter**
- Thread about platform
- Highlight first projects
- Share launch stats
- Engage community

#### 5. **Reddit**
- r/Bangladesh
- r/webdev
- r/learnprogramming
- Relevant tech subreddits

---

### Post-Launch (Ongoing)

#### Week 1-2
- Monitor feedback
- Fix bugs/issues
- Feature community projects
- Thank contributors

#### Month 1
- Weekly highlights
- Community showcase
- Share success stories
- Grow submission count

#### Ongoing
- Regular featured projects
- Developer spotlights
- Trending sections
- Community engagement
